What is a Paralegal?

A paralegal is a legal professional who assists lawyers in various aspects of their work. They perform tasks such as legal research, drafting legal documents, and organizing files and documents. Paralegals are a vital part of the legal team, and their work helps to ensure that legal proceedings run smoothly and efficiently.
