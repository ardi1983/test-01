Types of Jobs at Manchester Airport

Manchester Airport offers a diverse range of job opportunities to suit different skills and interests. From customer service to security and engineering, Manchester Airport has it all. Some of the popular jobs at the airport include:

Customer Service

If you love interacting with people and enjoy solving problems, a customer service job at Manchester Airport could be perfect for you. Customer service representatives at the airport assist passengers with their queries, provide information about flights and airport facilities and ensure a smooth passenger experience.

Security

The security team at Manchester Airport plays a vital role in ensuring the safety of passengers and staff. Security officers conduct checks on passengers and their luggage to detect any prohibited items and prevent any security breaches.

Cargo Handling

Manchester Airport is a hub for cargo operations, with tonnes of cargo passing through its doors every day. Cargo handlers are responsible for loading and unloading cargo from aircraft, ensuring that it is handled safely and efficiently.

Engineering

The engineering team at Manchester Airport is responsible for maintaining and repairing the airport's facilities and equipment. From electrical systems to runway maintenance, the engineering department plays a crucial role in keeping the airport running smoothly.

How to Apply for Jobs at Manchester Airport on Indeed

Indeed is a popular job search platform that can help you find a job at Manchester Airport. Here's how you can use Indeed to apply for jobs at the airport:

Create an Account

To apply for jobs on Indeed, you need to create an account first. It's a simple process that involves providing your email address and creating a password.

Upload Your Resume

Once you have created an account, you can upload your resume to your Indeed profile. This will make it easier for employers to find you and match your skills to their job requirements.

Search for Jobs

To find jobs at Manchester Airport on Indeed, simply search for "Manchester Airport" in the job search bar. You can also filter your search by job type, location, and salary.

Apply for Jobs

When you find a job that matches your skills and interests, click on the "Apply Now" button. This will take you to the employer's website, where you can submit your application.
