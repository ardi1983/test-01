Introduction

If you are a subscriber of Xfinity, you may have experienced problems with your remote control. It can be frustrating when your remote doesn't work, especially if you are in the middle of watching your favorite show. In this article, we will discuss the common reasons why your Xfinity remote is not working and how to fix it.

Reasons why Xfinity Remote is Not Working

There are several reasons why your Xfinity remote is not working. The most common reasons are:

1. Dead Batteries

The first thing you should check is whether your batteries are dead. If your remote isn't working, it could be because the batteries are dead or low. Replace the batteries and try again.

2. Obstruction

Another reason why your remote is not working is because of obstruction. If there is something blocking the path between your remote and your device, your remote will not work. Make sure there is nothing blocking the path.

3. Signal Interference

Signal interference can also cause your remote to stop working. If there are other devices in your home that use the same frequency as your Xfinity remote, it can cause signal interference. Move other devices away from your remote and try again.

How to Fix Xfinity Remote Not Working

Now that we have discussed the common reasons why your Xfinity remote is not working, let's talk about how to fix it.

1. Reset your Remote

The first thing you should do is reset your remote. To do this, press and hold the "Setup" button on your remote for three seconds. Then, enter the code "981". Your remote will reset and should start working again.

2. Pair your Remote

If resetting your remote doesn't work, you may need to pair it with your device. To do this, follow these steps: 1. Press and hold the "Setup" button on your remote for three seconds. 2. Enter the code "987". 3. Press the "OK" button. 4. Point your remote at your device and press the "Channel Up" button. Keep pressing the button until your device turns off. 5. Press the "Setup" button to save the code.

3. Contact Xfinity Customer Service

If none of the above solutions work, you may need to contact Xfinity customer service. They can help you troubleshoot the problem and may need to send a technician to your home to fix the issue.

Conclusion

In conclusion, having a non-functional Xfinity remote can be frustrating. However, there are several reasons why this may occur and a number of ways to fix it. From checking the batteries to resetting your remote, you should be able to get your Xfinity remote working again in no time. If all else fails, don't hesitate to contact Xfinity customer service for assistance.
