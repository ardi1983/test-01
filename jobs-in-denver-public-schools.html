Jobs In Denver Public Schools: A Comprehensive Guide

Introduction

If you're looking for a career in education, Denver Public Schools is a great place to start. With over 200 schools and more than 90,000 students, DPS is one of the largest school districts in the state of Colorado. This article will provide an overview of the types of jobs available in DPS and how to apply for them.

Types of Jobs

DPS offers a wide variety of career opportunities ranging from teaching positions to administrative roles. Some of the most common jobs include: - Teachers: DPS is always looking for certified teachers to join their team. Whether you specialize in elementary, middle, or high school education, there are plenty of opportunities available. - Support Staff: In addition to teachers, DPS also employs a large number of support staff including bus drivers, custodians, and administrative assistants. - Administrators: If you have experience in school administration or leadership, DPS may have a role that is right for you.

Qualifications

The qualifications for each position vary, but in general, DPS requires all employees to have a bachelor's degree and pass a background check. Teachers must also be certified to teach in the state of Colorado.

Application Process

To apply for a job in DPS, visit their website and search for open positions. You can filter by job type, location, and more. Once you find a job that interests you, submit your application and resume online.

Benefits

DPS offers a comprehensive benefits package to all employees including health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off. In addition, teachers may be eligible for loan forgiveness programs and other incentives.

Salary

The salary for each position varies based on experience and qualifications. However, teachers in DPS typically earn a starting salary of around $45,000 per year.

Professional Development

DPS is committed to providing ongoing professional development opportunities to all employees. Whether you're a new teacher or a seasoned administrator, there are plenty of opportunities to learn and grow within the district.

Diversity and Inclusion

DPS is dedicated to creating a diverse and inclusive work environment. They value the unique perspectives and backgrounds of all employees and strive to create a culture of respect and inclusion.

Conclusion

If you're passionate about education and want to make a difference in the lives of students, consider a career in Denver Public Schools. With a wide variety of job opportunities, competitive salaries, and comprehensive benefits, DPS is a great place to start your career in education. Visit their website today to learn more and apply for open positions.
