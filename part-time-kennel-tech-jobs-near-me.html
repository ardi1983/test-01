Part Time Kennel Tech Jobs Near Me: A Guide To Finding And Securing A Position In 2023

Introduction

If you're an animal lover looking for a part-time job, a kennel tech position might be perfect for you. Kennel techs work in animal care facilities, such as veterinary clinics, animal shelters, boarding kennels, and pet resorts. In this article, we will discuss how to find part-time kennel tech jobs near you and provide tips on how to secure a position in 2023.

Where to Look for Part-Time Kennel Tech Jobs

There are several places to look for part-time kennel tech jobs in 2023. The first place to start is online job boards, such as Indeed, Glassdoor, and Monster. You can also check out local classifieds, social media job groups, and company websites. Another option is to visit animal care facilities in person and ask if they have any part-time positions available.

Qualifications and Requirements

Most kennel tech positions require a high school diploma or equivalent, and some animal experience is preferred. However, some facilities may offer on-the-job training for entry-level positions. Other requirements may include excellent communication skills, the ability to lift heavy objects, and a willingness to work on weekends and holidays.

Job Duties

As a kennel tech, your main duties will include feeding, watering, and caring for animals in your facility. You may also be responsible for administering medication, cleaning kennels and cages, and providing basic grooming services. Additionally, you may be required to interact with customers and answer questions about their pets.

Tips for Securing a Part-Time Kennel Tech Job

1. Highlight your animal experience on your resume or application. 2. Be prepared to work weekends and holidays. 3. Dress professionally and arrive on time for your interview. 4. Demonstrate your passion for animal care and your willingness to learn. 5. Follow up with the facility after your interview to express your continued interest in the position.

Conclusion

In conclusion, part-time kennel tech jobs can be a great opportunity for animal lovers looking for flexible work. By utilizing online job boards, visiting facilities in person, and highlighting your animal experience, you can increase your chances of securing a position. Follow these tips and you'll be on your way to a fulfilling career in animal care.
