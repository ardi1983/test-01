News

Before applying for any government job, it is essential to read the eligibility criteria carefully. The government jobs in Maharashtra offer job security, good salary packages, and various other benefits. The employees are entitled to medical benefits, pension plans, and many more. Tutorial

If you are new to the government job application process, then here is a step-by-step tutorial to guide you through the process: Step 1: Visit the official website of the Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC). Step 2: Click on the 'Apply Online' button. Step 3: Fill in the required details such as name, date of birth, educational qualifications, etc. Step 4: Upload the scanned copies of your photograph and signature. Step 5: Pay the application fee through the online payment gateway. Step 6: Submit the online application form.
