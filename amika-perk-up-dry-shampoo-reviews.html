What is Amika Perk Up Dry Shampoo?

Amika Perk Up Dry Shampoo is a weightless, talc-free formula that absorbs excess oil, dirt, and impurities from your hair, leaving it clean and refreshed. It is formulated with natural rice starch, which helps to absorb oil and add volume to your hair without leaving any residue.

How to use Amika Perk Up Dry Shampoo?

Using Amika Perk Up Dry Shampoo is easy. Simply shake the can well, hold it 8-10 inches away from your hair, and spray on the roots. Let it sit for a few seconds, and then massage it into your scalp. Brush your hair to distribute the product evenly, and you are good to go.
